## About the Game

Aspen's Playground is a wave-based zombie survival shooter where you must survive increasingly difficult waves of the undead. Play solo or team up with friends in multiplayer co-op mode.

### Key Features

- **Wave-Based Survival** - Face endless waves of zombies that get stronger over time
- **7 Unique Weapons** - From pistols to laser guns
- **6 Zombie Types** - Each with unique abilities and behaviors
- **Epic Boss Fights** - Every 10 waves, face a powerful boss
- **Upgrade System** - Improve your weapons and abilities between waves
- **Multiplayer Co-op** - Team up with friends online
- **Global Leaderboards** - Compete for the highest scores
- **Cross-Platform** - Play in browser or download the desktop app

## Game Modes

### Single Player
Battle through waves solo. All zombies focus on you, but you keep all the upgrades and pickups.

### Multiplayer Co-op
Team up with other players online. Share the battlefield, revive fallen teammates, and survive together. Scores are tracked individually on the leaderboard.

## Quick Tips for New Players

1. **Keep moving** - Standing still is death
2. **Headshots deal bonus damage** - Aim for the head!
3. **Watch your ammo** - Reload during safe moments
4. **Use the shop wisely** - Prioritize damage and reload speed early
5. **Learn zombie sounds** - Each type has unique audio cues
6. **Grenades are powerful** - Save them for emergencies or groups
