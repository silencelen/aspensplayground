| SMG | Low | Very Fast | 30 | Sustained fire, runners |

## Weapon Strategies

### Early Game (Waves 1-5)
- **Primary**: Pistol → Shotgun/SMG
- Focus on accuracy, conserve ammo
- Learn headshot timing

### Mid Game (Waves 6-10)
- **Primary**: Assault Rifle
- **Secondary**: Sniper for Tanks
- Balance sustained damage and precision

### Late Game (Waves 11+)
- **Primary**: Laser Gun or Assault Rifle
- **Secondary**: Grenade Launcher for crowds
- **Backup**: Sniper for priority targets

### Boss Fights
- Laser Gun for sustained damage
- Grenades during safe windows
- Avoid Shotgun (too risky to get close)

## Upgrade Priority

Recommended upgrade order for weapons:

1. **Damage** - More damage = faster kills = less ammo used
2. **Reload Speed** - Less downtime = more shooting
3. **Fire Rate** - Only after damage is maxed
4. **Magazine Size** - Quality of life improvement

See [Upgrades](Upgrades) for full upgrade details.
