## Runner

**The Fast One**

```
Health:     ███░░░░░░░ (50 HP)
Speed:      █████████░ (Fast)
Damage:     ██░░░░░░░░ (10, 15 on leap)
Threat:     ★★★☆☆
```

### Behavior
- Sprints toward player
- **Leap Attack**: Jumps at player when 4-8 units away
- Leap deals 1.5x damage on landing

### Strategy
- Low health - focus fire to eliminate quickly
- Sidestep the leap attack
- SMG works well due to high fire rate
- Don't let them get behind you

### Appearance
- Reddish-brown skin
- Smaller, hunched posture
- Orange glowing eyes
- Torn clothing

## Crawler

**The Low Profile**

```
Health:     ████████░░ (80 HP)
Speed:      ██████░░░░ (Medium-Fast)
Damage:     ████░░░░░░ (20)
Threat:     ★★★☆☆
```

### Behavior
- Moves on all fours, low to ground
- Harder to hit due to low profile
- Faster than Normal zombies

### Strategy
- Aim lower than usual
- Shotgun effective due to spread
- Can slip under some obstacles
- Often missed when scanning for threats

### Appearance
- Dark green skin
- Crawling on hands and feet
- Bright green eyes
- Very low to ground

## Tank

**The Juggernaut**

```
Health:     ██████████ (350 HP)
Speed:      ██░░░░░░░░ (Slow)
Damage:     ███████░░░ (35, 70 on charge)
Threat:     ★★★★☆
```

### Behavior
- Slow but extremely tanky
- **Charge Attack**: When 6-12 units away, charges at 4x speed
- Charge deals 2x damage on hit
- Can stun player on hit

### Strategy
- Kite at medium range to avoid charges
- Sniper Rifle for safe damage
- Don't get cornered
- Priority target in groups
- Sidestep charges at the last moment

### Appearance
- Blue-grey armored skin
- Much larger than other zombies (1.5x scale)
- Blue glowing eyes
- Armored plating visible

## Spitter

**The Ranged Attacker**

```
Health:     ██████░░░░ (70 HP)
Speed:      ████░░░░░░ (Medium)
Damage:     ██░░░░░░░░ (12 per projectile)
Threat:     ★★★★☆
```

### Behavior
- Keeps distance from player
- **Retreats** when player gets too close (within 10 units)
- **Acid Spit**: Ranged projectile attack every 2 seconds
- Projectiles travel at medium speed

### Strategy
- Close the distance quickly
- Strafe to dodge projectiles
- High priority target - kill before they whittle you down
- Sniper Rifle counters them effectively
- Don't ignore them while fighting melee zombies

### Appearance
- Green-teal skin
- Elongated neck/head
- Teal glowing eyes
- Visible acid glands

## Exploder

**The Walking Bomb**

```
Health:     █████░░░░░ (60 HP)
Speed:      █████░░░░░ (Medium)
Damage:     ██████████ (50 explosion)
Threat:     ★★★★★
```

### Behavior
- Walks toward player like Normal zombie
- **Death Explosion**: Explodes on death
- Explosion radius: 5 units
- Explosion damages player AND other zombies

### Strategy
- Kill from a distance!
- Use the explosion to damage other zombies
- Never melee or shotgun at close range
- Sniper Rifle is ideal
- Watch for groups of Exploders

### Appearance
- Orange-brown bloated skin
- Slightly larger, swollen appearance
- Red-orange glowing eyes
- Visible pustules/growths

## Minion

**The Boss's Servants**

```
Health:     ██░░░░░░░░ (40 HP)
Speed:      ██████░░░░ (Fast)
Damage:     █░░░░░░░░░ (8)
Threat:     ★★☆☆☆
```

### Behavior
- Only appears during boss fights
- Summoned by the boss ability
- Fast but weak
- Swarms the player

### Strategy
- Quick to kill but distracting
- Don't ignore them - they add up
- Use AoE weapons (Grenade Launcher, grenades)
- Prioritize when boss is in cooldown phase

### Appearance
- Smaller than other zombies (0.7x scale)
- Purple-ish tint
- Basic zombie appearance

## Wave Composition

### Early Waves (1-4)
- Waves 1-2: Normal only
- Waves 3-4: Normal + Runners

### Mid Waves (5-9)
- Normal, Runners, Crawlers, Tanks
- Spitters and Exploders introduced at 7

### Late Waves (10+)
- All types in heavy numbers
- Boss every 10 waves
- Minions during boss fights

## Priority Targeting

When facing multiple zombie types:

1. **Exploders** - Kill at range before they get close
2. **Spitters** - Stop the ranged damage
3. **Runners** - Fast, can flank you
4. **Tanks** - High threat but slow, manageable
5. **Crawlers** - Moderate threat
6. **Normal** - Lowest priority

See [Boss Fights](Boss-Fights) for boss-specific strategies.
